space “Besish”

a place where the visitor can experience all negative emotions, spill them out learn to manage them and get psychological help
from specialist, each floor reflects a certain stage, through which a person must pass on the way to change, changing
aggression inside himself to peace.

3D flyover of the building space

The space looks like a crumpled piece of paper, thus illustrating
aggression and failed attempts.
The book has many corrections, running text, uneven blocks.

The checkroom in our building is very unusual - they are chairs on which
visitors pile their clothes one on top of the other in a pile.
On the 3rd floor there are 4 rooms with installations, each of which immerses
in a certain habit, in addition, the staircase to the next floor is also part
of the installation, it symbolizes "re-shoeing".

The lecture hall offers a variety of group trainings and lectures aimed
at developing self-control skills, managing emotions and increasing
psychological flexibility. Here you can familiarize yourself with advanced
methods from the field of psychology, get useful advice and learn how to apply them
in practice.

A specially designed room allows you to physically and emotionally
express your anger and frustration without repercussions.
Here visitors can live out negative emotions in an ecological way, namely
by taking out all their aggression on machinery, wooden crates
and utensils. This will help you to start working on yourself with a calmer
and more rational state of mind.
The blah room is also equipped with a fitting room and a comfortable waiting area with a sofa.
