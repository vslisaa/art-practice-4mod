book




the book is a guide to space, talking
about the idea of the building, its functions,
the author's solutions and the concept
of the work.

There are 5 sections in the book, each dedicated to a specific floor.
One floor is one stage in working on yourself and your character.

The illustrations in the book are renderings of 3d space. Everything is done
in minimalism, but the moving blocks, cross-outs and corrections create
a sense of tension.
